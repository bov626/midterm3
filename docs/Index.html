About Sydney Australia

Sydney is the best place to expereince Australia at its highest potential. With world class beaches for both swimming and surfing, amazing diverse resturants, unique nature outings and many other unforgettable experiences.

Best Beaches

Bondi Beach

Named the best beach in Australia for surfing and also has a Graffiti wall which is a great place to take instagram photos after swimming.

More Info
Marley Beach

Located in the upper royal national park this is a secluded unpatroled beach which can be a great place to get away from civilization and go for a swim.

More Info
Coogee Beach

This beach is great if you want to go to laid back pubs and bars. Also there is a beautiful jogging path which connects Coogee Beach to Bondi Beach.

More Info
Shark Island

Shark Island is well known for its amazing waves, but should be sought out by expereinced surfers only because because of its shallow waters and dry reef.

Things to Do

Wildlife Reserve

Visit a wildlife reserve where you will see animals you have never seen up close.

Go and see a show at the Sydney Opera house! Beautiful Scenery along with a show.

Great place to go if you have kids. Theres a ropes course and nature to explore.

Sydney Tower

The Tallest tower in the city which houses a rooftop resturant, perfect for a date.
